## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I switch plans?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

**Is there a free trial for Pro?**
Yes. All new accounts receive a 14-day free trial of the Pro plan, no credit card required.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Bank transfers are available for annual Enterprise contracts.
