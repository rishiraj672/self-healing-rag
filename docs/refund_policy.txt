Acme Corp Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Standard Returns
Customers may return any unused product within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Items must be in original packaging and accompanied by a valid receipt.
Digital products and software licenses are non-refundable once activated.

2. Defective Products
If your product arrives damaged or defective, contact support@acme.com within 7 days.
We will ship a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund including original shipping fees.
Photographic evidence of the defect must be submitted via the online portal.

3. Partial Refunds
Products returned between 31 and 60 days after purchase are eligible for a 50% store credit only.
No refunds are issued after 60 days from the date of purchase under any circumstances.

4. Refund Processing Time
Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
Credit card refunds may take an additional 3–5 business days to appear on your statement.
Store credit is applied to your account immediately upon approval.

5. Shipping Costs
Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a company error.
Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item is defective.
Acme Corp provides a prepaid return label for defective items only.
